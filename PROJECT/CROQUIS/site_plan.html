Overview

Purpose

For over 12 years, we've been growing high quality gourmet fruit. We take great pride in delivering the best fruit to our customers not only in Carlsbad, California, that is our home land, but to all our fruit lovers around the world!

Audience

The mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas and international customers.
