Target Audience

The target audience for this website is nearly everyone who has access to the internet, and either lives in one of the four areas covered or are going to or through them. If a person wants to know the weather in/around one of those areas, they are the targets for this website.

Persona 1:

Persona:

Tour Guide near Soda Springs

Fictional Name:

Alma Rome

Job Title:

Tour Guide Driver

Demographics:

  • 28 years old
  • Married
  • No children, but 2 older siblings
  • Going to school for a degree in Information Technology with an emphasis in networking

Goals and Tasks:

Wants to make sure that everyone in the tour has fun and is safe both with his driving and the weather.
He also loves to:

  • Go mountain biking with friends and family
  • Do picnics with her husband

Environment:

Between his job and his school, he doesn't have much time. With his major being IT he knows how to operate a computer and websites.

Quote:

"My life is so crazy right now, and i'll live even more!."

Scenarios:

  • Alma has a scheduled tour starting in about 15 minutes, she needs to see the weather to help her be aware of possible trail conditions so she can drive safer, and she can let the tour group know to prepare for the weather during the tour.
  • SHe and a few friends are planning to go on a mountain bike trail, to make sure that everyone is as safe as possible Alma can look at the weather to be aware of trail conditions and/or prepare for the weather conditions during their trail riding.
  • To save money, she rides her bike to school. Before she leaves the house each time, she checks the weather to see if he should bring her coat, or to ride slower and leave early in case of ice.
  • She and her husband planned a date night going to have a picnic dinner and watch a movie outside. She needs to check the weather to see if they need to bring coats or move their picnic/movie undercover.
  • Winter is coming and her car still doesn't have winter tires, she needs to know which day would be the safest day to take her car in to get winter tires on.

Persona 2:

Persona:

grandma

Fictional Name:

Barbara Thompson

Job Title:

Retired

Demographics:

  • 76 years old
  • Widowed
  • 7 children
  • 27 grandchildren

Goals and Tasks:

She loves to plan get-togethers with all the family.
She enjoys:

  • Going on short walks (especially with family)
  • Hosting "Dinner-At-Grandma's"
  • Playing with her grandchildren (though she can't do it as much as she would like to)

Environment:

She took a computer basics class a while ago, but with tech advancing so fast she is still behind with it all. So simple websites and apps, are almost a necessity.

Quote:

"Can you help me with my phone again, I think I broke it..."

Scenarios:

  • She wants "Dinner-At-Grandma's" to be outside this time she needs to check the weather to see if it will be a comfortable evening outside.
  • With her morning walks, Barbra checks the weather to decide what would be the most comfortable to wear in that temperature.
  • She is planning a play-day with her grandchildren and needs to know what the weather is going to be like to plan accordingly.
  • She and her kids are planning a 3 day get-together and she wants to make it the best 3 days. She checks the weather to see what the best 3 consecutive days would be to plan their activity.
  • Her grandchildren want to go sledding, and she wants to take them. She needs to figure out which day(s) would have the safest snow for sledding.
